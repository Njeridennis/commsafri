Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple designs typically take 3-5 business days, while complex branding projects may take 2-3 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide design revisions?

Yes, we include up to 3 rounds of revisions in our standard packages to ensure the final design meets your expectations. Additional revisions can be accommodated if needed.

What file formats do you deliver?

We provide designs in various formats including AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, and JPG. For print projects, we ensure high-resolution files suitable for professional printing.

Do you handle installation services?

Yes, we provide complete installation services for signage, vehicle wraps, and exhibition setups. Our experienced team ensures professional installation and quality results.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa), cash payments, and checks. We typically require 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.

Do you work with clients outside Nairobi?

Absolutely! We serve clients throughout Kenya and can arrange delivery or installation services. We also work remotely for design-only projects with clients worldwide.
