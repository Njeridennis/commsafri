Our Story

Founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize branding

In 2010, Sixth Sense Dimension was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to offer cost-effective branding, signage, and promotional services without compromising on quality or creativity. What started as a small creative studio has evolved into a comprehensive below-the-line advertising agency that has transformed the visual identity of hundreds of businesses across Kenya.

Our founders recognized a gap in the market - businesses needed creative solutions that were both impactful and affordable. They understood that great branding shouldn't be a luxury reserved for large corporations, but an accessible tool for businesses of all sizes to communicate their unique value propositions.

Over the past 13+ years, we've grown from a team of passionate designers to a full-service creative agency, but our core mission remains unchanged: to master the art and science of conveying your ideas and concepts through exceptional visual solutions.
